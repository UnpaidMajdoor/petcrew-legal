# Privacy Policy

**Pet Crew** — operated by **Sanket Maharana, based in Bengaluru, India**.

**Effective date:** July 1, 2026
**Contact for data requests:** maharana.mds92@gmail.com

_This is the Pet Crew Privacy Policy, written to align with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. It is written in plain English and is not a substitute for individual legal advice._

---

## 1. Who we are

Sanket Maharana is the **Data Fiduciary** responsible for personal data processed through Pet Crew. If you have any questions or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at **maharana.mds92@gmail.com**.

## 2. Data we collect

When you create an account and use Pet Crew, we collect:

- **Name** — to identify you in the community;
- **Phone number** — for authentication and, optionally, for sharing on your posts;
- **Email address** — for authentication and account communication;
- **Date of birth** — to verify that you are 18 or older;
- **City / location** — your general location; and, for rescue and adoption posts, the specific coordinates you choose to attach;
- **Profile photo and cover photo** — if you choose to add them;
- **Content you create** — posts, images, captions, comments, and chat messages;
- **Pet details** — information you add about your pets.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive financial data. Please do not share financial or other sensitive personal information through chat.

## 3. Why we use your data (purpose)

We process your data only for clearly defined purposes:

- to **provide the pet community features** — posts, rescue, adoption, Q&A, chat, and (post-launch) sitter services;
- to **verify your age** (18+ requirement);
- to **authenticate you** and keep your account secure;
- to **operate, maintain, and improve** the service;
- to **respond to abuse reports** and enforce our Terms;
- to **comply with applicable law**.

We rely on your consent, given when you create an account and use these features, and on our need to provide the service you have requested.

## 4. How your data is shared

- **Public content** — your name, profile photo, and the posts, listings, comments, and Q&A you publish are visible to other users of Pet Crew. Do not post anything you wish to keep private.
- **Phone number** — your phone number is **private by default**. It is shared on a post **only when you explicitly turn on phone sharing** for that post. You can turn it off at any time.
- **Chat** — messages are visible to the users in the conversation. We do not routinely monitor them (see Terms, section 9).
- We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 5. Third-party processors

We use trusted third parties (Data Processors) to run the service:

- **Firebase (Google)** — authentication (phone and Google sign-in);
- **Supabase** — database and file storage for your account and content;
- **Google Maps / Places (Google)** — location selection and mapping for posts.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments.

## 6. Your rights under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a summary of the personal data we hold about you;
- **Correction** — correct inaccurate or incomplete data (much of this you can edit directly in the app);
- **Erasure / deletion** — delete your account and associated data;
- **Data portability** — request a copy of the data you have provided, in a usable format;
- **Grievance redressal** — raise a complaint about how your data is handled;
- **Withdraw consent** — withdraw consent for processing (this may mean you can no longer use parts of the app).

To exercise any of these rights, contact **maharana.mds92@gmail.com**. You can delete your account and its data at any time from **Settings → Delete Account** within the app.

## 7. Data retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your **account is active**. When you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data **within 30 days**, except where we are required to retain certain information to comply with a legal obligation, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

## 8. Children

Pet Crew is **not intended for anyone under 18**. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18. If we learn that an account belongs to a person under 18, we will terminate it and delete the associated data.

## 9. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including access controls and the use of reputable infrastructure providers. No system is perfectly secure; please help protect your account by keeping your sign-in method safe.

## 10. Governing law

This Privacy Policy and any dispute relating to it are governed by the laws of **India**.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated Policy with a new effective date and, for material changes, provide reasonable notice through the app.
