# Delete Your Pet Crew Account

Pet Crew allows you to delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

## Steps to delete your account

1. Open the Pet Crew app
2. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation)
3. Tap the **settings icon** (top right)
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Type "DELETE" to confirm
6. Tap **Delete account**

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your profile (name, phone, email, date of birth, city, avatar, cover photo)
- All posts you created (rescue posts, adoption listings, community posts, Q&A)
- All comments and likes
- All chat messages
- All bookmarks
- All uploaded images

## What may be retained

- Analytics and logs required for legal compliance may be retained in anonymized form
- Data required by applicable law may be retained for up to 30 days

If you cannot access the app, email **maharana.mds92@gmail.com** with your registered phone number or email, and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.
