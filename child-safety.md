# Pet Crew Child Safety Standards

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026
**Contact:** maharana.mds92@gmail.com

Pet Crew is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

## Age Requirements

Pet Crew is restricted to users **18 years and older**. Date of birth verification is required at signup, and accounts found to belong to persons under 18 are terminated.

## Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Pet Crew:

- Content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers children
- Grooming, luring, or predatory conduct toward minors
- Sharing of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
- Solicitation of minors for meetings or personal contact
- Content that objectifies or sexualizes minors

Violations result in immediate account termination and reporting to relevant authorities including the **National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC)** and Indian authorities under the **POCSO Act, 2012**.

## Reporting Mechanisms

**In-app**: All posts and messages can be reported via the report button in the app. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

**Email**: Direct reports to maharana.mds92@gmail.com

## Detection and Response

We use a combination of user reporting and content moderation to identify prohibited content. Confirmed CSAM is:

1. Removed from the platform immediately
2. Reported to NCMEC and Indian authorities
3. Preserved for law enforcement review as required by law

Accounts involved in prohibited conduct are permanently banned and reported.

## Compliance

Pet Crew complies with:

- **POCSO Act, 2012** (India)
- **IT Rules, 2021** (India)
- **DPDP Act, 2023** (India)
- Google Play Store child safety standards
